Our Services at Extreme Fitness Gym & Studio

1. Strength & Conditioning:

Whether you’re a beginner or a serious lifter, our weight training area is fully equipped with modern free weights, squat racks, benches, and resistance machines to help you build muscle and improve overall strength.

2. Full Gym Access:

Step into a high-performance fitness space designed for real results. Our gym is equipped with:

  • Advanced strength training machines
  • Extensive free weights section
  • State-of-the-art cardio machines (treadmills, elliptical trainers, bikes, stair masters)
  • CrossFit rigs & functional training tools (battle ropes, TRX, kettlebells)
  • Open space for circuit training and stretching

3. Group Fitness Classes:

Get stronger, leaner, and more energized with our wide variety of group classes. These sessions are led by certified instructors and are great for burning calories while having fun.Our most popular group classes include:

  • Zumba & Dance Fitness – High-energy music and moves to torch calories
  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) – Fast-paced, fat-burning workouts
  • Yoga & Power Yoga – Improve flexibility, mental clarity, and core strength
  • Pilates – Focus on posture, balance, and body control
  • Step Aerobics & Bootcamps – Dynamic, full-body conditioning workouts

4. Personal Training:

Our personal training programs are designed for individuals who want one-on-one attention and a fully customized fitness plan. Your coach will assess your current fitness level, discuss your goals, and create a results-oriented plan that includes:

  • Personalized workout routines
  • Nutritional coaching
  • Constant progress tracking
  • Injury prevention and form correction

5. Nutrition & Diet Consultation:

Exercise alone won’t get you the results you want — proper nutrition is key. Our certified dieticians and fitness coaches will help you:

  • Understand your body type and metabolism
  • Create tailored meal plans (veg/non-veg/keto/low-carb, etc.)
  • Get supplement guidance (if needed)
  • Learn sustainable healthy eating habits

6. Ladies-Only Fitness Programs:

We offer a private, empowering space for women to train comfortably with personalized or group workouts led by female trainers. Special focus is given to:

  • Weight loss & toning
  • Hormonal balance & PCOD-friendly workouts
  • Pre/Post-natal fitness
  • Strength & endurance building

7.Kids & Teen Fitness:

Designed to build a strong foundation of fitness in children and teens, these fun, age-appropriate programs improve:

  • Flexibility and balance
  • Strength and coordination
  • Discipline and confidence
